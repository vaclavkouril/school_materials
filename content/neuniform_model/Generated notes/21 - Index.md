# 21 - Index

## Lecture 22 April 2020

This lecture continues the discussion of small-depth circuit classes and focuses on:

- arithmetic in $NC^1$,
- summing many integers in logarithmic depth,
- the class $AC^0$,
- counting arguments showing that $AC^0$ cannot compute all Boolean functions,
- parity and modular counting gates,
- the beginning of the Razborov--Smolensky polynomial method.
